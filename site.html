About Drachma

Drachma is an ERC-20 token on the Ethereum blockchain. The first of its kind, created with a mission to deliver a deflationary token with value not bound simply by its investment.

Our goal is, as cryptocurrencies evolve that a great initiative is taken to allow profits from investments and crypto development to drive philanthropic efforts around the world.
